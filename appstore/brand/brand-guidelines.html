Brand Guidelines · premium dark / gold

Travel together, organized.

The mark, explained

Primary lockup — gold mark on near-black (#0A0A0A). Use this as the default.

The globe ringed with trip-coordination icons (chat, calendar, places, transport, media) says the whole story in one glance: a group, a destination, and every moving part held in one place. Gold is the through-line — warmth and premium confidence against a calm near-black field.

Rule: gold is reserved for primary CTAs and brand elements — never a full background, never on bright surfaces. Tailwind mapping: bg-black / bg-gray-950 surfaces, text-amber-400 / bg-amber-500 gold, text-white headlines.

Type system

Headline — Bold, white, tight.
Descriptor — gold, supporting the verb.
Body — regular, gray, calm and spacious.

The App Store / marketing pattern is a one-word white action verb + a gold descriptor (e.g. PLAN — your next adventure). Headlines tight and bold; body relaxed and quiet.

Verbal identity

One-liner: Plan trips, chat with your group, split expenses, get an AI travel concierge, and keep your itinerary, map, and memories in one shared place.

Travel together, organized. Less chaos. More coordination. Your whole trip, in one place. Group travel, handled.
Voice — do
  • Short, declarative, benefit-first.
  • Action verbs; calm confidence.
  • Sound like a well-traveled friend who has the logistics handled.
Voice — don't
  • No exclamation spam.
  • No loud / gimmicky hype.
  • No gold on bright backgrounds.
