# Chravel — Founder Video (≈30–45s)

Production-ready script + shot list for the **Founder Video** skill
(prompt in [`../PIKA_FOUNDER_KIT.md`](../PIKA_FOUNDER_KIT.md), Part C #4).
For investors and customers: who we are, what we made, why it matters.

- **Format:** 9:16 vertical (social) or 16:9 (pitch) — script is aspect-agnostic.
- **Palette:** dark/gold lower-thirds, gold `#c49746`, white type, Chravel wordmark.
- **Tone:** calm, confident founder-to-camera. A well-traveled friend, not a pitchman.

## Arc → script (VO / to-camera)

| Beat | Time | Line | B-roll / lower-third |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Problem** | 0–8s | "Every group trip starts the same way — a dozen chat threads, a lost spreadsheet, and nobody sure who paid for what." | Cuts of messy group chats, scattered notes; lower-third: founder name + "Founder, Chravel" |
| **What it is** | 8–22s | "So we built Chravel. One shared place to plan the trip, chat with your group, split every expense, and get an AI travel concierge that actually knows your itinerary." | App in hand — dashboard, chat, expense split, concierge answering |
| **Why now / why us** | 22–35s | "Travel is back, groups are bigger, and the tools never caught up. Chravel keeps the whole trip — itinerary, map, and memories — in one calm place, on every phone." | Map + memories gallery; multiple phones showing the same trip |
| **CTA** | 35–42s | "If you travel with people you love, you need this. Chravel — travel together, organized." | Gold wordmark end card + "Download on the App Store" |

## Lower-thirds
Dark bar with gold rule; white name, gold role. Keep on screen ~3s, ease in/out.
Match the brand sheet type: bold white + gold accent.

## Score
Sparse, warm pad under the problem; lifts subtly at "So we built Chravel"; resolves
on the end card. Music never competes with the VO.

## Deliverables
- `founder.mp4` (≈30–45s)
- `founder-poster.png` (end-card wordmark frame)
- This script (the VO of record)

## Capture notes (if shooting live instead of generating)
- Eye-level, soft key light, near-black or warm-dark background to stay on-brand.
- Mic close; quiet room. Deliver lines conversationally — pauses are fine.
- Get clean app screen-captures separately (use the populated trips, not empty states)
  so b-roll matches the App Store screenshots in `../screenshots/marketing/`.
