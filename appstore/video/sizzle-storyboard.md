## Score
Warm, modern, mid-tempo electronic with a single confident beat drop at beat 2
(≈2.0s). No vocals. Resolve cleanly on the end card — let the wordmark breathe.

## Caption style
White verb (bold, tight) + gold descriptor underneath. Snap on, brief hold, snap
off. Never more than ~4 words on screen at once. Safe margins: keep text within the
center 80% (avoid notch + home indicator).
