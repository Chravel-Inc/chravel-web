Chravel keeps your group trip planning in one place.

Plan faster with shared itineraries, group chat, polls, to-dos, map-based discovery, and trip expense tracking built for real coordination.

Key features:
- Shared trip timelines and collaborative scheduling
- Real-time group chat for every trip
- Polls and tasks to align group decisions
- Places, maps, and saved travel links
- Group expense tracking and payment coordination
- AI Concierge to help plan, organize, and summarize
- Photo and media sharing for trip memories

Built for every type of travel group:
- Friends and families planning vacations
- Destination events and group celebrations
- Organizers running logistics across larger teams

Privacy and account controls:
- Account deletion available in-app and on the web
- Authenticated access to collaborative trip content
- Support and policy pages available at chravel.app

Subscription notes:
- Some features require a paid plan
- Subscription management depends on platform billing capabilities

Chravel is a travel coordination app. It is not a booking marketplace or online travel agency.
